Regions Manaslu Tsum Naar Phu Tilicho Annapurna

Treks Tour of Manaslu - Tsum Valley - Rupina La - Larkya Pass

3 treks from 20 to 28 days
- March to May - September to December
level 3/4 and 4/4

It is the alternative to the Tour of the Annapurnas less traveled, with less equipment therefore more interesting. If you have a few more days there are variants by the very isolated Rupina or by the Tsum valley a Tibetan enclave of Buddhist culture for a trip in another time. At the end of the trek there is the passage of the Larkya Pass at 5200 m as a culmination.

Detail

Trek Naar Phu Lake Tilicho and Naar Thorong Pass

2 20-day treks
- April and May - mid-September to December
level 4/4

The Trekking of the Naar and Phu Valleys and the bypass of Lake Tilicho offer a wide variety of landscapes. The area of ​​Naar Phu immerses us in another era and another culture because of the proximity of Tibet. That of Tilicho leads us, through a journey in an arid universe, in high mountains with the Tilicho lake - grandiose.

Detail

Treks in the Annapurna Valley Thorong Pass

2 treks of 14 and 16 days
March to May - mid-September to january
level 3/4

These treks will bring you, for a first come in Nepal, all that Nepal and the Nepalese can offer you, the Annapurna to "reach of hands", kindness and welcome. Treks with varied landscapes of rice paddies in the high valleys at the foot of the imposing faces of Annapurna. Without forgetting the ascent of the Thorong Pass at 5400 m which finishes the trek in beauty.

Detail

Treks in the Annapurna Valley South Tuesday Himal and Annapurna Shrine

2 treks of 10 and 12 days
March to May - mid-September to December
level 2/4

Treks of short duration, not difficult with slow climbs in altitude. Various landscapes of the greenery of the departure end at the foot of the summits of the South Annapurna, Machapuchare and Mardi Himal.

Regions Khumbu Everest

Treks in the Khumbu - Renjo Pass - Kalapatar - Nangpa The - Everest Base Camp

3 treks from 15 to 24 days
- April and May - mid-September to December
level 3/4 - 4/4

We offer 3 treks of different durations and difficulties from Lukla. Khumbu is the mythical massif linked to the presence of many summits over 8000 m including the Everest. A Khumbu away from the world is the Nangpa La trek on the way Tibetans traveled with Nepal. All these treks are beautiful forays at high altitudes.

Detail

The Other Khumbu - Three Passes Renjo Pass - Chola - Kongma

2 treks from 18 to 23 days
- April and May - mid-September to December
level 3/4 - 4/4

We offer 2 treks from Phaplu or Jiri in SoloKhumbu. This is another way to discover the Khumbu that allows better acclimatization and so enjoy this high-altitude trip. Moreover, the landscapes between Phaplu or Jiri and Lukla offer another view of Khumbu less touristy. Then we take full advantage of the crossing of the 3 passes of Khumbu because we had more time to acclimatize our organism to the altitude.

Detail

The Tashi Lapsa between Khumbu and Rolwaling

20 days
- April and May - mid-September to December
level HC

rom a departure from Jiri we join the Khumbu valley in 4 days. Then we will head towards Tashi Lapsa. It is necessary to plan two camps, one at 5600 m. After passing the Tashi Lapsa descent into the Rolwaling valley to return to nearby Jiri.

Régions Dolpo Haut Dolpo Mustang Dhaulagiri

Trek Dolpo

2 treks 18 to 48 days
- April and May - July to mid-November
level 3/4 and HC

The Dolpo and Phoksumdo Lake trekking is a trek in the heart of Tibetan culture. This is the road that leads to Tibet used by the Yaks who carry goods that Nepalese and Tibetans exchange. The walk along Lake Phoksundo is extraordinary with its overhanging trail cut into the rock.

Detail

Top Dolpo treks - Phoksundo lake - Rara lake

3 treks 31 to 36 days
- April-May and September November
HC level

We offer 3 treks at the Dolpo High: Phoksundo Lake - High Dolpo then Lake Rara - High Dolpo and sacred lakes Rara and Phoksundo. You like the scenery, you seek the immersion in unfrequented countries, we advise you the treks of the High Dolpo. Peeled lands, monasteries, lakes, Yaks caravans, fantastic color landscapes, etc. it is the Dolpo High.

Detail

Mustang Treks - by Tanggye - Mustang Naar Valley

3 treks of 14, 17 and 23 days
- March to December
level 2/4 and HC

Ancient Tibetan enclave nowadays attached to Nepal the Mustang is a colorful mineral desert. The Mustang is both a high place of Buddhism and a passage between Tibet and Nepal of salt caravans. At Lo Mantang the capital of the Mustang is transported to another era.

Detail

Dhaulagiri Tour Trek

18 days
- April and May - September to mid-November
level 4/4

The trekking on the tour of Dhaulagiri is little developed. Indeed, from the 3rd day of trek there is no more lodge you will be autonomous. After a start in the greenery from the camp of the Italians it is a mountain world. A trek like in the beginning of the trekking in Nepal without lodge in autonomy with your team. It is a committed trek.

Régions Makalu Kangchenjunga

Trek Kangchenjunga

24 days
- April and May - mid-September to November
level 4/4

The Kangchenjunga trekking takes place in an uncrowded area due to its distance from Kathmandu. So there are few tourists so that if you are looking for authentic and secluded it is a trek to do.

Detail

Makalu Trek

23 days
- April and May - mid-September to November
level 3/4

The first part of the trekking takes place in the rice fields. Different ethnic groups coexist and offer us their own culture. Then the valley of the Barun will lead us to nearly 4800 m at the base camp Hillary of the Makalu.

Detail


Régions Kathmandu Langtang

Treks in Langtang - lakes Gosainkund - Kangja La

3 treks from 12 to 22 days
- January to May - September to December
level 2/4 and 4/4

The inhabitants of Langtang are natives of Tibet they are Sherpas. The passage from the Lauribinayak Pass to 4600 m bypassing the Gosainkund lakes is simply fabulous. Crossing the Langtang through the Kangja La Pass is a must.

Detail

Cultural trek Kathmandu and its valley

11 days
All year round
level 1/4

This "cultural Kathmandu" tour will take you to the heart of this fabulous city of Kathmandu and its surroundings. Visits to Buddhist temples, museums, neighborhoods, with a French-speaking Nepali guide. Then a small trek of 4 days in the hills around the capital.
