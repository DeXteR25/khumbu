Treks Tour of Manaslu - Tsum Valley - Rupina La - Larkya Pass

3 treks from 20 to 28 days
- March to May - September to December
level 3/4 and 4/4

It is the alternative to the Tour of the Annapurnas less traveled, with less equipment therefore more interesting. If you have a few more days there are variants by the very isolated Rupina or by the Tsum valley a Tibetan enclave of Buddhist culture for a trip in another time. At the end of the trek there is the passage of the Larkya Pass at 5200 m as a culmination.

Detail

Trek Naar Phu Lake Tilicho and Naar Thorong Pass

2 20-day treks
- April and May - mid-September to December
level 4/4

The Trekking of the Naar and Phu Valleys and the bypass of Lake Tilicho offer a wide variety of landscapes. The area of ​​Naar Phu immerses us in another era and another culture because of the proximity of Tibet. That of Tilicho leads us, through a journey in an arid universe, in high mountains with the Tilicho lake - grandiose.

Detail

Treks in the Annapurna Valley Thorong Pass

2 treks of 14 and 16 days
March to May - mid-September to january
level 3/4

These treks will bring you, for a first come in Nepal, all that Nepal and the Nepalese can offer you, the Annapurna to "reach of hands", kindness and welcome. Treks with varied landscapes of rice paddies in the high valleys at the foot of the imposing faces of Annapurna. Without forgetting the ascent of the Thorong Pass at 5400 m which finishes the trek in beauty.

Detail

Treks in the Annapurna Valley South Tuesday Himal and Annapurna Shrine

2 treks of 10 and 12 days
March to May - mid-September to December
level 2/4

Treks of short duration, not difficult with slow climbs in altitude. Various landscapes of the greenery of the departure end at the foot of the summits of the South Annapurna, Machapuchare and Mardi Himal.
